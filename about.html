How Pal Forge IT Started

Pal Forge IT Solutions was built out of a direct frustration with how most IT providers operate. The pattern was always the same — businesses would call for help only after something broke, pay emergency rates to get patched back together, and go right back to running on systems one bad day away from the next incident.

The businesses being hurt most were small and mid-sized operations with real revenue, real employees, and real exposure — who did not have the budget for an enterprise IT department but were being treated as afterthoughts by large MSPs chasing bigger contracts. Pal Forge IT was built specifically for those businesses.

What We Do Differently

From day one, we built our service delivery around a security and monitoring stack that most MSPs our size do not offer. We invested in enterprise-grade tools across endpoint protection, email security, compliance automation, and backup — so our clients have access to the same technology that large enterprise IT departments rely on.

We also committed to response time standards we hold ourselves to in writing. A 15-minute guaranteed response is not a marketing claim. It is a service level we built our entire operation around.

What We Deliver

Enterprise Capability. Without the Enterprise Price Tag.

We have invested in the same categories of tools that Fortune 500 IT teams use, deployed and managed so your business gets the protection without the overhead.

Endpoint Security and Threat Detection

Every managed device is protected with AI-driven threat detection and active response. Attacks are contained automatically, often before you notice anything is wrong.

Email Security and Domain Protection

Your email domain is locked down so nobody can send fraudulent emails pretending to be your business. Phishing and spoofing attacks are blocked before they reach your team.

Backup and Disaster Recovery

Your data is backed up, encrypted, and tested regularly. If something happens — ransomware, hardware failure, accidental deletion — we have you back online in hours, not days.

Compliance and Audit Readiness

Continuous compliance monitoring gives you a real-time view of where you stand on HIPAA or other regulatory requirements. We manage the program so you are never caught off guard.

Network Infrastructure

Business-grade networking deployed correctly — segmented, monitored, and managed remotely. Your connection is reliable and your network does not become a security liability.

Cloud and Productivity Management

Your Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and cloud infrastructure managed end to end — migrations, security hardening, user provisioning, and ongoing administration.
