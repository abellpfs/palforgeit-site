Overview

A Security Camera System That Works When You Need It To.

Consumer-grade cameras and DIY NVR setups fail in predictable ways — poor image quality, storage that fills up and stops recording, systems that go offline unnoticed. When an incident happens, footage is either missing or unusable.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

How long is footage stored?
Storage duration depends on camera count, resolution, and motion activity. We typically configure 30, 60, or 90 day retention based on your requirements.
Can we access footage remotely?
Yes. We configure secure remote access so you can view live feeds and recorded footage from a smartphone, tablet, or computer.
Do you work with existing cameras?
It depends on what you have. Many consumer-grade systems are not compatible with commercial NVR infrastructure. We assess your existing hardware and give you an honest recommendation.
Is this available as a standalone service?
NVR installation is available as a standalone project. Ongoing monitoring is included with a managed IT plan or available as a separate support agreement.
