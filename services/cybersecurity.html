Overview

Layered Security That Stops Threats Before They Reach You.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

Do small businesses really need this level of security?
Yes. Attackers do not discriminate by company size. In fact, small businesses are frequently targeted precisely because they are assumed to have weaker defenses. The cost of a breach — downtime, data loss, regulatory exposure — far exceeds the cost of prevention.
What is the difference between Huntress and SentinelOne?
SentinelOne is an endpoint protection platform that blocks known and unknown threats in real time. Huntress adds a managed detection and response layer on top — a team of security analysts actively hunting for threats that automated tools may have missed. They work together.
What happens when a threat is detected?
Our team is immediately notified. We isolate the affected system if necessary, investigate the scope of the threat, remediate it, and provide you with a full incident report.
Do you cover Microsoft 365 environments?
Yes. Huntress includes coverage for Microsoft 365, monitoring for compromised accounts, suspicious sign-ins, mailbox forwarding rules, and other common attack vectors in cloud environments.
