Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

Do small businesses really need this level of security?
Yes. Attackers do not discriminate by company size. Small businesses are frequently targeted because they are assumed to have weaker defenses. The cost of a breach far exceeds the cost of prevention.
What is the difference between Huntress and SentinelOne?
SentinelOne blocks known and unknown threats in real time. Huntress adds a managed detection layer on top — analysts actively hunting for threats automated tools may miss. They work together.
What happens when a threat is detected?
Our team is immediately notified. We isolate the affected system if necessary, investigate, remediate, and provide you with a full incident report.
Do you cover Microsoft 365 environments?
Yes. Huntress includes coverage for Microsoft 365, monitoring for compromised accounts, suspicious sign-ins, mailbox forwarding rules, and other common cloud attack vectors.
