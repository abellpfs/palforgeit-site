Overview

A Backup You've Never Tested Is Not a Backup.

Most businesses have some form of backup in place. Very few have actually tested whether it works. A ransomware attack or hardware failure is not the time to find out your backup has been failing silently for six months.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

How often is our data backed up?
Endpoints typically back up continuously or daily. Servers can be backed up as frequently as every 15 minutes for critical workloads. We set frequency based on your business requirements.
Where is backup data stored?
In secure, encrypted cloud storage through Druva. We can also configure local backup targets for faster on-site recovery.
How long does it take to restore from a backup?
Individual file restores can happen in minutes. Full system restores take longer but are executed against a documented plan to make the process as fast as possible.
Does Microsoft 365 back up our data automatically?
No. Microsoft maintains platform uptime but does not protect against accidental deletion or ransomware. You need a separate backup solution for your Microsoft 365 data.
