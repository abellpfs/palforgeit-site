Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?
Our guaranteed response time is 15 minutes. That applies to tickets submitted through any channel — phone, email, or the client portal — during all hours of the day.
Do you require long-term contracts?
No. We do not lock clients into multi-year agreements. Our goal is to earn your business every month by delivering consistent results.
What size businesses do you work with?
We work best with businesses in the 10 to 100 seat range. If you are outside that range, reach out and we can talk through whether we are a good fit.
Can you take over from our current IT provider?
Yes. We handle the transition process, including documentation, credential collection, and onboarding, with minimal disruption to your team.
