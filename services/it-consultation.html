Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

How is this different from getting a quote from an IT vendor?
Vendors have a financial interest in selling you their products. Our consultation is independent — recommendations are based entirely on what is right for your business.
How long does a consultation engagement take?
An initial assessment and roadmap for a 20 to 50 user business typically takes two to three weeks from kickoff to final deliverable.
Do we need to become a managed IT client after the consultation?
No. A consultation engagement stands on its own. Many clients go on to work with us on a managed basis, but there is no obligation.
We already have an IT provider. Can you consult without replacing them?
Yes. We can provide an independent assessment of your environment without requiring any changes to your existing relationships.
