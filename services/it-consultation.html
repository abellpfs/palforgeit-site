Overview

Get an Outside Perspective Before Making a Major IT Decision.

Whether you are planning a significant infrastructure upgrade, evaluating vendors, preparing for rapid growth, or trying to understand why your current technology is not performing the way it should — a structured IT consultation gives you clarity before you commit resources.

We come in without a vendor agenda. Our recommendations are based on what makes sense for your specific business, your budget, and where you are trying to go. If what you have is working, we will tell you. If it is not, we will show you exactly what needs to change and why.

Consultation engagements can be standalone projects or the starting point for an ongoing managed IT relationship. Either way, you leave with a clear picture of your environment and a actionable plan.

What an IT Consultation Covers.

01

IT Infrastructure Assessment

A thorough review of your current environment — hardware, software, networking, cloud services, and security posture — documented in plain language with clear findings.

02

Technology Roadmap Development

A prioritized, phased plan for your IT environment that aligns with your business goals, your budget, and a realistic timeline. No jargon, no vague recommendations.

03

Vendor Evaluation and Selection

Objective analysis of technology vendors and platforms relevant to your needs. We evaluate options against your specific requirements and help you avoid costly mistakes.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

How is this different from just getting a quote from an IT vendor?
Vendors have a financial interest in selling you their products and services. Our consultation is independent — we are not reselling specific hardware or software in this engagement, so our recommendations are based entirely on what is right for your business. We may recommend products we also deploy, but we explain why and you can verify the reasoning independently.
How long does a consultation engagement take?
It depends on the scope. An initial IT assessment and roadmap for a business with 20 to 50 users typically takes two to three weeks from kickoff to final deliverable. Larger or more complex environments take longer. We scope each engagement individually and give you a clear timeline before we start.
Do we need to become a managed IT client after the consultation?
No. A consultation engagement stands on its own. Many clients do go on to work with us on a managed basis, but there is no obligation. You get the deliverables and can implement the recommendations however you choose.
We already have an IT provider. Can you consult without replacing them?
Yes. We can provide an independent assessment of your current IT environment and your provider's work without requiring any changes to your existing relationships. Some businesses use this as a way to verify they are getting value from their current provider.
