Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

We are already using Microsoft 365 — can you take over management?
Yes. We audit your existing environment, identify security gaps, and take over ongoing administration without requiring a full migration.
How long does a cloud migration take?
Small businesses with 10 to 25 users typically complete a migration in one to two weeks. Larger environments require more planning.
Is our cloud data backed up?
Not by default. Microsoft and Google maintain their platforms but are not responsible for recovering deleted or ransomware-encrypted data. We include cloud-to-cloud backup in Security Plus and Compliance Ready plans.
Can you support a hybrid environment?
Yes. We manage hybrid environments and can help you plan a phased move to full cloud.
