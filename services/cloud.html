Overview

The Right Cloud Setup Makes Every Part of Your Business Run Better.

Moving to the cloud is not just about cutting server costs. Done correctly, it gives your team the flexibility to work from anywhere, improves collaboration, strengthens your security posture, and reduces the maintenance burden on your IT environment.

Done incorrectly, it creates a fragmented, poorly secured environment that is harder to manage than what it replaced.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

We are already using Microsoft 365 — can you just take over management?
Yes. We can audit your existing environment, document what you have, identify any security gaps or misconfigurations, and take over ongoing administration without requiring a full migration.
How long does a cloud migration typically take?
It depends on the size and complexity of your environment. Small businesses with 10 to 25 users can typically complete a migration in one to two weeks. Larger environments take longer and require more planning.
Is our cloud data backed up?
Not by default. Microsoft and Google are responsible for keeping their platforms running, but they are not responsible for recovering data that was accidentally deleted, overwritten, or encrypted by ransomware. We include cloud-to-cloud backup in our Security Plus and Compliance Ready plans.
Can you support a hybrid environment with both on-premises and cloud systems?
Yes. Many businesses run a mix of on-premises servers and cloud services. We manage hybrid environments and can help you plan a phased move to full cloud if that is the direction you want to go.
